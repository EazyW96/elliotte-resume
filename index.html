UBID Online Auction System

Course: SWE 6633 – Software Project Planning & Management (Summer 2024)
Team Members: Hunter Blake, Mekonnen Kindo, Connor Bland, Elliotte Wideman
Overview: This 6-week team project focused on planning and building an online auction platform to help a traditional auction business expand online. The system supports item listings, bidding, user registration, and reporting. Students followed the full software development lifecycle, from requirements gathering to implementation, using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL.
